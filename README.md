# PartyFinder

A social media platform for discovering and rating nightlife events in your area.

## About

PartyFinder helps you find the best parties, bars, and clubs near you. Whether you're looking for a house party, checking out a new bar, or finding the hottest club in town, PartyFinder makes it easy to discover events and see what others think before you go.

## Key Features

- **Discover Events** - Browse parties, bars, and clubs near your location
- **Rate & Review** - Share your experience and read reviews from other users
- **RSVP** - Let others know you're going and see who else is attending
- **Host Events** - Create your own party listing with all the details (location, cost, age, drinks, etc.)
- **Real-Time Updates** - See current attendee counts and live updates from events
- **Map View** - Visualize all events on an interactive map

## Why PartyFinder?

Never waste a night at a dead party or overpriced club again. See real ratings from real people and make informed decisions about where to spend your night out.
